About Me

I'm a Game Designer with focus on level design, technical implementation and cognitive perception. I love to create games that motivate, immerse and bring adventures to the players.
Currently I work at University of Mannheim in Germany as a Software Developer working mainly with OOP PHP, SQL and JavaScript.

Next month, I am finishing my Master degree at the Faculty of Cognitive Science at the University of Osnabruck.

In the past few years, I was involved in several DevOps and Unity3D projects at university, inspiring me to advance in this amazing field. I am inventive and focused on the future.
