HeadHunter - Wild West Train Adventure


Project Goal:

The goal of this project was to create a Single Player First Person Shooter game with a focus on level design. The player is introduced to a stolen train full of murderers and bandits, and must move forward from the back of the train to its front to arrest or eliminate the main bandit. The game also features different endings based on the player's decisions, as well as the option to play in stealth mode.

▶ Description

In HeadHunter, the player learns the basics of movement, jumping, shooting, vaulting, and ladder climbing in the first two wagons of the train. The game also introduces collectibles, health, and ammo mechanics, and allows the player to fight enemies using guns or melee attacks. The game can also be played in stealth mode, with different ways to avoid contact with enemies or loud fights.
One of the unique features of HeadHunter is its multiple endings. The player can achieve a common ending where they defeat all enemies and arrest the main bandit, but have to jump into a lake to survive because they broke the train stop mechanics. Alternatively, the player can achieve a good ending where they not only defeat all enemies and arrest the main bandit, but also let go of any hostages and train workers that they find. They still have to jump off the train to survive, but the bandit is successfully arrested. Finally, the player can achieve a bad ending where they fail to arrest the bandit, and the train crashes.
